THE STORY

Reports of a strange orange syrup-like substance are appearing all around the city. Emerging from the background of past events, these underdog FBI agents are forced to go undercover — again. Do they have what it takes to get past all the extra work and get to the bottom of the pickle jar the city is being held under?
